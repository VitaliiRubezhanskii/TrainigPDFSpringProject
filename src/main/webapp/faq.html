Frequently asked questions

How does SlidePiper make my business more successful?

SlidePiper lets you focus on your most interested customers. At-best 10-30% of your leads will actually qualify. What if you could talk to these first? Now imagine you could do this all the time.

Is there a long installation process?

SlidePiper is a SaaS product (software as a service). Therefore there no installation process. Just register for your trial and you're ready to go.

Does my customer need to install anything?

No installation is needed on the customer side, just a web browser.

How does SlidePiper recommend the next steps?

We use sophisticated machine learning algorithms to analyze and learn your customer's behavior. We then weigh out the possible alternatives, and suggest the best next step for you to take in your specific sales process.

Is it hard to use?

We've worked hard so you don't need to. Our solution is very simple to use. It's practically a one touch solution. Just call and we'll quickly fill you up on everything.

Do I have to make new presentations and marketing materials?

No. We work with your normal presentations and marketing materials that you already have. You may use PowerPoint, Word, videos, PDFs and even pictures.

What if I don't have a CRM?

We can set you up with our simple built-in CRM and you are ready to go. Additionally, the system has the option of working indepdenently, without a CRM.

Where do I start?

Welcome to SlidePiper! Call us or drop us an email and we'll fill you up on the specifics. It's easy!
