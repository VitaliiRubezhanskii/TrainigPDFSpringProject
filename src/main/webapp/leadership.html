Leadership
“the people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do.” - Steve Jobs

Sivan Bender - Founder & CEO

SlidePiper is basically a solution to a problem that I encountered in my day-to-day work. I am the customer and therefore know what the perfect solution has to provide. I love working with and helping people. I believe strongly that anything can be done with the right team, the right conviction and plain putting in the effort to do it! As an entrepreneur I have been able to use and master many skills gained in both my education and my previous enterprise experience in the business world. My strongest points are being able to lead and hold a creative vision while breaking it down into actionable tasks. As an Industrial & Management Engineer with strong physics and CS background I also like to get my "hands dirty" technically when I need to.

Shauli Daon - CTO & Product Architect

Shauli is our technical expert, although he is involved in all aspects of the product development cycle. He is in charge of building the product and strives to make the end user experience both seamless and feature-rich. He is also responsible for our state-of-the-art machine learning recommendation engine. This engine provides our clients with valuable insights regarding when and how to approach their customers. Shauli studied for a PhD in Chemical Physics at the Weizmann Institute of Science.
